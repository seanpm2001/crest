Support

 

Crest is released as open source software and comes with no commercial support.

But since we want to ensure success and recognize that Crest consumers might fall into a range of roles - from developers that are steeped in the conventions of open-source to customers that are more accustomed to VMware commercial offerings, we offer several methods of engaging with the Crest team and community.

For the Crest community, feel free to join our Slack channel
